# **App Name**: Vitanalyze

## Core Features:

- Personalized Profile Setup: Users create an in-depth profile including health goals, dietary preferences, restrictions, activity levels, and initial biometrics to tailor their experience.
- AI Meal & Exercise Planner: An AI tool generates daily and weekly customized meal plans with recipes and exercise routines based on the user's profile, progress, and specified goals.
- Food & Activity Logging: Intuitive interface allowing users to easily log their daily food intake, track macronutrients, and record various physical activities.
- Progress & Goal Tracking: A dashboard displaying visual representations (charts, graphs) of the user's progress toward their health goals, including weight, activity metrics, and nutritional intake.
- AI Coaching Insights: An AI tool that analyzes logged data to provide personalized feedback, identify patterns in habits, and offer actionable advice for optimizing health and nutrition.
- Curated Healthy Resources: Access to a library of healthy recipes with detailed nutritional information and various exercise routines to supplement personalized plans.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A balanced and natural green (#529452) for a feeling of growth and health.
- Background color: A serene and light greenish-white (#F2F5F2) for a clean and refreshing interface.
- Accent color: A vibrant, energetic chartreuse (#B6EB3D) for highlighting key actions and achievements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly readable qualities, ensuring clarity across all content.
- Use minimalist, outline-style icons for a clean and modern look, representing nutrition, fitness, and data tracking concepts.
- Implement a clean, card-based layout with ample white space to ensure focus on content and provide a visually uncluttered user experience.
- Incorporate subtle, smooth transitions and micro-interactions, particularly when logging data or updating progress, to provide positive user feedback.
